Glued-Shoe Basketball
A basketball game where the shoes are glued to the floor.
You have five players on the court - but nobody can move.
The ball starts with your in-bounder.
Click on other players to pass.
Click on the basket to shoot.
Players closer to the basket are more likely to score.
If a pass or shot is blocked, the other team might score.
The game is played to 7 points. Good luck!
